Covid-19 Guidelines

At WCM, your health and safety is our top priority. We have the following covid-19 measures in place:

  • Fully vaccinated status mandatory to enter the mall premises.
  • Masks mandatory inside the mall.
  • Maintain social distancing inside the mall(2ft distance).
  • Do not form large groups at one place.

About

One of the oldest and largest malls in Canada. It covers a total area of 10 million square feet. It is located near the prime business district downtown of the city. It has 100+ different stores such as clothing stores, book shops, food outlets, cosmetics shops, footwear stores and many more. It also has a day-care for children so that parents can shop freely without any worries.
